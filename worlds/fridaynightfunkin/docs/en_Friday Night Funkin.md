# Friday Night Funkin'

## Where is the settings page?

The player settings is inside of the AP Mode of Mixtape Engine.

## What does randomization do to this game?
All of your songs will be locked from the start, with one starting song. There will also be tickets in which are required to play your goal song.


## What is the goal of Friday Night Funkin' when randomized?
The goal is to beat your Victory Song by unlocking it and collecting enough tickets.


## Which items can be in another player's world?
Songs, Items, and Misc. Filler can be scattered around. Sometimes, it's literally a "Lonely Friday Night"!


## What is considered a location check in Friday Night Funkin'?
Depending on your settings, beating a song counts as 2 checks, with an optional 3 notes randomly in each song.

## What does another world's item look like in Friday Night Funkin'?
There is no particular appearance, except for notes which may have a notably different color than others. 



## When the player receives an item, what happens?
If you recieve an item, a notification will appear. For songs, Freeplay will refresh with the new song, while other items may be put into a queue for the next song.
